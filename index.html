About

Yvette Kondoh

Data Science | Data Stories | Data Products

I have 3+ years experience building end-to-end credit risk and predictive models for assessing new business risk and behavior risk in the agritech and fintech industry.
I work remotely as a Data Analytics Scientist with Superfluid Labs, a Pan-African data analytics company with teams in Germany, Ghana and Kenya.
I have hands-on experience gleaning and communicating actionable insights from data to stakeholders, demonstrating through analysis, the business impact of deploying a candidate model in production, and deploying models in production in conjunction with software engineers.
Being a pioneer data scientist in my current job, I have been involved in shaping the standard operating procedures and developing internal tools for successfully executing projects from inception to model deployment.
My daily toolstack usually includes Python, Git and Google Suite but I can quickly learn and easily adapt to a new tool if required to execute a project.
I hold a Bachelors degree in Actuarial Science from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, Ghana and a Masters degree in Applied Statistics from Loyola University Chicago, USA.
